Project Details

Ceci

IDP 3 Assignment

September-December 2024

Team

Ogbeide Osarieme

Role

Brand Designer

Duration

14 Weeks

Ceci is a makeup brand offering a curated collection of essential beauty products,
featuring a luxurious micellar cleansing oil, a vibrant glossy lipstick, and a lightweight
translucent setting powder. The brand focuses on delivering high-performance cosmetics
that provide effortless application, superior quality, and a commitment to enhancing natural
beauty across diverse skin tones and types.

The Challenge

During the project, I struggled to develop a cohesive visual identity for ceci, particularly
in selecting the perfect color palette. After numerous iterations and design explorations, I
ultimately resolved the challenge by creating a sophisticated gradient that effectively captured
the brand's essence and provided a unique, modern aesthetic.

The Solution

I experimented with various color combinations and design techniques, eventually creating a
gradient that perfectly captured the brand's essence and aesthetic.

The Outcome

The final design featured a striking gradient that enhanced CECI's visual identity, resulting in
a cohesive and modern look that resonated with the target audience and effectively showcased the
brand's products.
